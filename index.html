What is tabata?


Tabata is a type of HIIT (High Intensity Interval Training) workout. The 4 minute workout consists of a 10 second rest period and 20 seconds of exercise, repeated 8 times. Tabata workouts are a short, intense way to improve both your cardio and strength.
